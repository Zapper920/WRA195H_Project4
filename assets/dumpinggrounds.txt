MSU, typical of a formal institution, requires learning a mix of both the contemporary and traditional modes of instruction. All AAHD (Art, Art History, and Design) students are required to take courses in the fundamentals of art and the principles of design. Following this prerequisite, students are able to take more complex courses in typography and graphic form.
